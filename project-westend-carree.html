Revitalization Westend Carrée

Sustainability certification, redesigned common areas, and improved outdoor facilities of a five- to eight-story office complex offering around 31,000 m² of rental space in Frankfurt's prestigious Westend. The main focus of the conversion lay on creating spatial clarity and enabling easy orientation, befitting the prestigious location and high tenant expectations.

What now appears effortless and simple is the result of determined work — a light-filled, open entrance area where large-format glass panels create a puristic clarity, replacing what was once an arbitrarily assembled building complex. Located on the popular Grüneburgweg, the complex combines centrality and renown into an exceptional office location.

Project type: Commercial

Location: Frankfurt am Main

Timeline: 2021 – 2022

GFA: 4,670 m²

Budget: €13M

Role: Project Lead

Project Scope

The revitalization focused on achieving sustainability certification (DGNB/LEED), complete redesign of common areas including lobbies, corridors and shared facilities, improvement of outdoor facilities including courtyards and entrance zones, and modernization of building systems to meet current energy efficiency standards.
